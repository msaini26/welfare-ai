About Us

Welfare-AI's primary objective: To observe and improve the physical and emotional wellbeing of its users.

Welfare-AI was inspired by the need for accessible health resources. In a time where individuals are the most isolated, we created a hack that provides physical and emotional guidance using machine learning, in hopes of providing users with pieces of optimism and help reinforce healthy habits for the body and mind.

Emotional Tracker

Welfare-AI observes the facial expressions of the users, and guages their mood. If the user is sad, the AI will provide small suggestions that may help boost the user's mood!

Bytes of Optimism

Welfare-AI makes sure to provide small optimistic quotes, and useful wisdom in times of need.

The Music and Me

Feeling sad? Welfare-AI makes sure to provide some serotonin-boosting Spotify playlists for the user!
